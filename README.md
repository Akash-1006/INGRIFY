# **Problem Statement**

Product ingredient labels are often complex, technical, and difficult to interpret.
This lack of transparency makes it hard for users to identify harmful or allergenic ingredients, leading to poor or unsafe product choices.

# **Solution**

Ingrify simplifies ingredient analysis by converting complex ingredient lists into easy-to-understand insights, enabling users to make informed decisions across multiple product categories.

# **Features**
Ingredient Scanning
Scan product labels using the camera or upload images from the gallery.

Ingredient Analysis
View detailed explanations of ingredient purpose, benefits, and potential side effects.

Allergen & Safety Alerts
Identify ingredients that may cause irritation, allergies, or health concerns.

Multi-Category Support
Works with skincare, cosmetics,and food products.

User-Friendly Interface
Simple, clean UI delivering fast and easily understandable results.

# **Tech Stack**

Frontend: Android (Kotlin + XML)

Backend: Python (Flask)

API Communication: Retrofit

AI / NLP: Ingredient classification and explanation engine


# **Application Flow**

User scans or uploads a product label

Text is extracted from the image

Ingredients are identified and analyzed

Results are displayed with safety and allergen insights

 
# **Target Users**

Health-conscious individuals

People with allergies or sensitive skin

Consumers seeking transparency in products

Parents and caregivers
